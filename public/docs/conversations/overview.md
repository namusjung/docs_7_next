# Conversations

The Conversations section gives you full visibility into how your agent is performing in the real world. From here you can review chat logs, track analytics, manage leads, and identify where your agent needs improvement.

{% section id="what" title="What's in Conversations" %}

The Conversations section is divided into three main areas:

**Activity** — A log of every chat session your agent has handled. Browse, filter, and search through real conversations. Use this to spot recurring questions, identify knowledge gaps, and flag responses that need improvement.

**Analytics** — Metrics dashboard showing conversation volumes, user feedback scores, topic distribution, and sentiment trends over time. Use this to measure performance and track improvement after training updates.

**Leads** — A record of contact information captured through your agent's lead collection forms. Export or connect to your CRM.

{% /section %}

{% section id="access" title="Accessing Conversations" %}

1. Log in to the [7en Dashboard](https://app.7en.ai)
2. Open your agent
3. Click **Conversations** in the left navigation panel

All data is scoped to the selected agent. If you manage multiple agents, switch between them using the agent selector at the top of the page.

{% /section %}

{% section id="data-retention" title="Data & Privacy" %}

Conversation logs are stored securely and accessible only to members of your workspace. Depending on your plan, logs are retained for 30–365 days.

{% callout type="info" title="Data starts recording after plan activation" %}
Conversation data is only captured after you have an active subscription. Free trial conversations may not appear in the analytics view.
{% /callout %}

{% /section %}
